This is a game about, you guessed it, shooting stuff. It is set in space.

CONTROLS:
Because of the limitations of reading keys from stdin, the following controls may seem strange.

Movement:
	W: Move forward
	A: Turn left
	Q: Turn left and move forward
	D: Turn right
	E: Turn right and move forward

Shooting:
	SPACE: Shoot
	SHIFT+KEY: Carry out action ordered by KEY while shooting
